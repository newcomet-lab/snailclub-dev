WELCOME TO SILLY SNAILS!

We came up with the snail games, a semiannual event hosted on Solana blockchain, the games feature 7 unique skill-based games that once successfully completed will secure the holder a Victory barnacle for their NFT and a share of the XYZ grand prize! Upon minting your silly snail, you are thrown into the first game with your NFT Snail avatar, steady your hands because you only get one shot per Snail to compete! You are given the choice to play your NFT or save it as a Collectable. Each snail is completed with metadata containing their traits, rarities and generation statistics.

THE SNAIL GAMES

What are the games?

Minting a silly Snail is your ticket into the Snail Games, Solanas premiere compete to win online game! When you are mint your silly snail you have the option to keep it as an unplayed snail or you can risk damaging it in the snail games, if your skills are( up to par you can escape the games with a victory snail), you may slide away with damage ranging from a cracked shell to a pile of ash...

Prize Pool

If you can maneuver the games successfully you will be rewarded with a Victory Barnacle, a spot in the winner's circle and your share of an XYZ grand prize! Good Luck!

CONTENDERS CIRCLE

To enter the Contenders Circle , one must posses at least one Silly Snail. Possession will grant you special access to:

  • Your wallet address whitelisted.
  • Access to Contenders Circle private chatroom.
  • Access to the Snail Games.

WINNER'S CIRCLE

The Winners Circle is made up of contenders that have successfully completed each stage of the first Snail Games. Securing a spot in the Winners Circle will grant you special access to:

  • The Winners Circle Private Chat Room.
  • A victory Barnacle air dropped to your wallet.
  • An invitation to the Winners Circle party, a live event hosted in luxury.
  • A cut of the prize pool.
  • Bragging rights.

FAQ

How can I buy a Silly snail?

Mint a snail on launch day through our website portal, connect your wallet and proceed to the mint portal.

When is mint day?

Keep your eyes on our Twitter and Discord!

What are the snail games?

The snail games are a test of intellect and skill in a series of challenges; successful completion of the games will land you a spot in the Winners Circle.

How many snails can one person own?

You may mint one snail at a time, however there is no limit to the amount of snails an individual can possess.

How many traits are available?

There are a combined total of 136 traits available.

What traits are rare?

True trait rarities will be discovered once all snails have been minted.

Will there be a community fund?

X% of resales will go to the community fund, this fund will be used as a treasury to help us grow in a sustainable way.

Is there a prize for the snail games?

If you make it through the snail games your wallet will be in the winners circle! This entitles your wallet to a share of the 100 solana Grand prize! Your payout will be calculated depending on how many people end up winning.

Will there be a secondary market

Yes, We are working with Magic Eden as a main secondary marketplace!

Are there second sale royalties?

There will be an 6.9 % distribution of resales sent to wallets holding their Silly Snail, distributions are set high to help us succeed in the long term! Prize pools gotta come from somewhere!
